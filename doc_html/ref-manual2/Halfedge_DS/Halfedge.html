Requirements for a Halfedge

Definition

A halfedge must store pointers to the next halfedge and its opposite halfedge. It optionally stores pointer to its incident vertex and incident facet. Type tags indicate whether these member functions are supported. Figure  depicts the relationship between a halfedge and its incident halfedges, vertices, and facets. The is_border() predicate is mandatory, but may return always false. A halfedge is an oriented edge between two vertices. It is always paired with its counterpart that has the opposite direction. They are mutually linked with the opposite() member function.
