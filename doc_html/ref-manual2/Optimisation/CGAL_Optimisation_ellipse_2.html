2D Optimisation Ellipse (CGAL_Optimisation_ellipse_2)

Definition

An object of the class CGAL_Optimisation_ellipse_2<R> is an ellipse in the two-dimensional Euclidean plane E2. Its boundary splits E2 into a bounded and an unbounded side. Note that the ellipse can be degenerated, i.e. it can be empty, equal to a single point or equal to a segment. By definition, an empty CGAL_Optimisation_ellipse_2<R> has no boundary and no bounded side, i.e. its unbounded side equals the whole plane E2. A CGAL_Optimisation_ellipse_2<R> equal to a single point p or equal to a segment s, resp., has no bounded side, its boundary is {p} or s, resp., and its unbounded side equals E2-{p} or E2-s, resp.
