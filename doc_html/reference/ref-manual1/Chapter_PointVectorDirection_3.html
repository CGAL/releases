Conversion between Points and Vectors

As in the two-dimensional case you subtract a point p from the symbolic constant CGAL_ORIGIN to obtain the locus vector of p. In order to obtain the point corresponding to a vector v you simply have to add v to CGAL_ORIGIN. See Section for an example.
